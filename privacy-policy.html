Tiger Dashcam 隐私政策

生效日期：2025年1月1日

Tiger Dashcam（以下简称"我们"或"本应用"）尊重并保护用户的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

1.1 位置信息

  • 收集内容：GPS 坐标、速度、海拔等位置数据
  • 收集目的：在录制的视频中嵌入位置信息，帮助用户记录行车路线
  • 存储方式：仅存储在您的设备本地，不上传到任何服务器

1.2 摄像头和麦克风

  • 收集内容：视频和音频数据
  • 收集目的：录制行车视频
  • 存储方式：仅存储在您的设备本地，您可以随时查看和删除

1.3 设备信息

  • 收集内容：设备型号、操作系统版本、广告标识符
  • 收集目的：用于广告展示和应用性能优化
  • 第三方服务：通过 Google AdMob 收集

2. 信息使用方式

  • 提供核心功能：录制、存储和管理行车视频
  • 改进用户体验：优化应用性能和稳定性
  • 展示广告：通过 Google AdMob 展示相关广告（您可以通过观看激励广告获得无广告体验）

3. 数据存储和安全

  • 本地存储：所有录制的视频、音频和位置数据仅存储在您的设备上
  • 不上传服务器：我们不会将您的录制数据上传到任何远程服务器
  • 用户控制：您可以随时在应用内删除任何录制文件
  • 权限管理：您可以在设备设置中随时撤销应用权限

4. 第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

4.1 Google AdMob

  • 用途：展示广告
  • 收集数据：设备广告标识符、IP 地址、设备信息
  • 隐私政策：https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您发现儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们将立即删除。

6. 您的权利

  • 访问权：您可以随时查看应用内存储的所有数据
  • 删除权：您可以随时删除录制的视频和相关数据
  • 撤销权：您可以在设备设置中撤销应用权限
  • 卸载权：卸载应用将删除所有本地存储的数据

7. 隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并在应用内通知用户。继续使用本应用即表示您接受更新后的隐私政策。

8. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • 应用名称：Tiger Dashcam
  • 开发者：Tiger Car Recorder Team
  • 联系方式：通过 Google Play 商店页面联系

Tiger Dashcam Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Tiger Dashcam ("we", "our", or "the app") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

  • What we collect: GPS coordinates, speed, altitude, and other location data
  • Purpose: Embed location information in recorded videos to help users track driving routes
  • Storage: Stored only on your local device, not uploaded to any server

1.2 Camera and Microphone

  • What we collect: Video and audio data
  • Purpose: Record dashcam videos
  • Storage: Stored only on your local device, you can view and delete at any time

1.3 Device Information

  • What we collect: Device model, OS version, advertising identifier
  • Purpose: Ad display and app performance optimization
  • Third-party service: Collected via Google AdMob

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality: Record, store, and manage dashcam videos
  • Improve user experience: Optimize app performance and stability
  • Display ads: Show relevant ads via Google AdMob (you can earn ad-free experience by watching rewarded ads)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: All recorded videos, audio, and location data are stored only on your device
  • No server upload: We do not upload your recording data to any remote server
  • User control: You can delete any recorded files in the app at any time
  • Permission management: You can revoke app permissions in device settings at any time

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Data collected: Device advertising identifier, IP address, device information
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data stored in the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete recorded videos and related data at any time
  • Revocation: You can revoke app permissions in device settings
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page and users will be notified in the app. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • App Name: Tiger Dashcam
  • Developer: Tiger Car Recorder Team
  • Contact: Via Google Play Store page
