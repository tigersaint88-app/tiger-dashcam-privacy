Tiger Dashcam - Data Deletion Guide

Important: Tiger Dashcam stores all data (videos, location information, settings) locally on your device only. We do not upload any data to remote servers. This means you have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Delete Individual Videos (Recommended)

Steps:
  1. Open Tiger Dashcam app
  2. Go to Video Library tab
  3. Find the video you want to delete
  4. Long press on the video or tap the menu icon
  5. Select Delete
  6. Confirm deletion

Result: The selected video and its associated location data will be permanently deleted from your device.

Method 2: Delete All Videos

Steps:
  1. Open Tiger Dashcam app
  2. Go to Video Library tab
  3. Tap the Select All button (if available)
  4. Or select multiple videos
  5. Tap Delete
  6. Confirm deletion

Result: All selected videos and their data will be permanently deleted.

Method 3: Clear All App Data (Complete Deletion)

Steps:
  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and select Tiger Dashcam
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data or Clear Storage
  6. Confirm

Result: All videos, settings, and app data will be completely deleted.

Method 4: Uninstall App (Complete Removal)

Steps:
  1. Long press the Tiger Dashcam app icon on your home screen
  2. Select Uninstall
  3. Confirm uninstallation

Or through device settings:

  1. Open Settings → Apps
  2. Find Tiger Dashcam
  3. Tap Uninstall

Result: The app and all associated data will be completely removed from your device.

Data Storage Information

  • Videos: Stored in your device's internal storage or SD card
  • Location Data: Embedded in video files, stored locally only
  • Settings: Stored in app's local database
  • Server Upload: None - we do not upload any data to servers

What Gets Deleted

When you delete data using the methods above, the following information is permanently removed:

  • All recorded videos
  • GPS location information associated with videos
  • App settings and preferences
  • All local cache and temporary files

Need Additional Help?

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance:

  • App Name: Tiger Dashcam
  • Developer: Tiger Car Recorder Team
  • Contact: Through Google Play Store app page
  • Response Time: Within 2-3 business days

Note: Since all data is stored locally on your device and not on our servers, you have immediate and complete control over data deletion without needing to contact us.

Tiger Dashcam - 数据删除指南

重要提示： Tiger Dashcam（老虎行车记录仪）将所有数据（视频、位置信息、设置）仅存储在您的设备本地。我们不会将任何数据上传到远程服务器。这意味着您完全控制您的数据，可以随时删除。

如何删除您的数据

方法1：删除单个视频（推荐）

操作步骤：
  1. 打开 Tiger Dashcam 应用
  2. 进入 视频库 标签
  3. 找到您想删除的视频
  4. 长按视频或点击菜单图标
  5. 选择 删除
  6. 确认删除

效果： 所选视频及其关联的位置数据将从您的设备中永久删除。

方法2：删除所有视频

操作步骤：
  1. 打开 Tiger Dashcam 应用
  2. 进入 视频库 标签
  3. 点击 全选 按钮（如有）
  4. 或者手动选择多个视频
  5. 点击 删除
  6. 确认删除

效果： 所有选中的视频及其数据将被永久删除。

方法3：清除所有应用数据（完全删除）

操作步骤：
  1. 打开手机 设置
  2. 进入 应用 或 应用管理
  3. 找到并选择 Tiger Dashcam
  4. 点击 存储
  5. 点击 清除数据 或 清除存储空间
  6. 确认

效果： 所有视频、设置和应用数据将被完全删除。

方法4：卸载应用（完全移除）

操作步骤：
  1. 长按主屏幕上的 Tiger Dashcam 应用图标
  2. 选择 卸载
  3. 确认卸载

或通过设备设置：

  1. 打开 设置 → 应用
  2. 找到 Tiger Dashcam
  3. 点击 卸载

效果： 应用及所有关联数据将从您的设备中完全移除。

数据存储信息

  • 视频文件： 存储在设备内部存储或SD卡中
  • 位置数据： 嵌入在视频文件中，仅本地存储
  • 设置信息： 存储在应用本地数据库中
  • 服务器上传： 无 - 我们不上传任何数据到服务器

删除的内容

使用上述方法删除数据时，以下信息将被永久移除：

  • 所有录制的视频文件
  • 与视频关联的GPS位置信息
  • 应用设置和偏好
  • 所有本地缓存和临时文件
